Priest of Dawn:
[ Rewards/Benefits for Winning the Seven Signs Competition ]
1. Rewards for collecting seal stones: Each member of the winning group can claim their reward in Ancient Adena by visiting the applicable priests in each village during the seal validation period. The reward is in proportion to the number of seal stones recovered. Ancient Adena may be used to buy various consumable items, such as CP Potion and SP scroll, from a priest on the team. Ancient Adena can also be traded with Mammon's trader or Mammon's blacksmith.
2. Rewards from the Festival of Darkness: All the parties participating in the Festival of Darkness must pay a participation fee. The fees will be stored and paid out as a reward. For each grade level, the party that obtained the highest score and belongs to the winning group will receive the whole amount. The reward will be paid in the form of Ancient Adena. The reward can be claimed from the festival guide, located at the Oracle of Dawn during the seal validation period.
3. Trading seal stones for Ancient Adena: The members of the winning group can trade their seal stones for Ancient Adena. To do this, visit a Priest of Dawn, located in each village.
4. Controlling the Seal of Avarice: - The Lords of Dawn will take possession of the eight necropolises and those not belonging to the winning team can no longer visit the necropolises. However, if the outcome is a draw, then both teams will have access to the necropolises. - You can meet Lilith, the black sacred mother. She is the archenemy who sleeps in the Disciples Necropolis. - You can trade with Mammon's trader, who often appears in the necropolis. He accepts Ancient Adena, and possesses rare and valuable items such as Scroll: Enchant Armor for all grades, A grade crystals, gemstones and various dyes.
5. Controlling the Seal of Gnosis: The Lords of Dawn will take possession of the six catacombs and those not belonging to the winning team can no longer access the catacombs. If the fight ends in a draw, both teams can access the catacombs. - You will have the privilege of trading with Mammon's blacksmith, who frequents the catacombs. He accepts Ancient Adena, and offers such special services such as trading C or D grade weapons for better ones and bestowing special powers to A grade weapons. - Pay Ancient Adena to a Priest of Dawn located in each village if you wish to be teleported to one of the major hunting grounds. You will find an Orator of Revelations in each village, giving support magic to the members of the Lords of Dawn and a Preacher of Doom casting curses on the members of the Revolutionaries of Dusk.
6. Controlling the Seal of Strife: -The lord of each castle can now hire fearsome mercenaries to defend them. -This should improve the defense capability of all castles and castle gates somewhat. -The cost of upgrading all castle gates and castle walls will be reduced. -The maximum tax rate will increase.
