[ Learn Magic ]

Congratulations, you've reached level 7!
Now you can learn new magic and strengthen your existing magic as well! To do this, speak with the seer in the village. Follow the arrow above your head to find the seer.
To learn new magic, you must purchase an amulet at a magic items shop. The seer will tell you which amulet to get.
A certain quantity of skill points (SP) are required for you to learn a skill. SP increases each time you kill a monster.
SP information is available in the Character Information Window. Click the face-shaped icon on the bottom-right of the screen or type ALT+T to access the Character Information Window.
