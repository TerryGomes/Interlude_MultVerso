Newbie Guide:
For adventurers of your level, the Marshlands, south of the village, are very popular hunting grounds, as is the area around the School of Dark Arts. You can effectively increase your level by defeating marsh zombies or skeleton hunters.
One rule that always holds true -- the farther from the village you venture, the stronger the enemies you will meet. Therefore, as your level increases, you should hunt farther away from the village. If travel time is becoming an inconvenience, you may use the gatekeeper to take you to the outpost where Tyra lives. This is in the southern part of the forest, where strong monsters can be found.
