Newbie Guide:
You are ready to undertake more challenging quests. Sentinel Knight Alberius of the Temple of Eva is striving to create a fever remedy. Seek him out, for he will greatly reward you if you help him.
Hierarch Asterios, also of the Temple of Eva, is troubled by the contamination of the village water system. He will have need of your aid.
Also, east of the Temple of Eva, Alshupes is engaged in a study of giant spiders. Speak to him, and you can help him increase his collection.
