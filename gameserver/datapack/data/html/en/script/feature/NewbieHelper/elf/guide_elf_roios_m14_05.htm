Newbie Guide:
Many of the villagers are in need of your help, and the quests they offer can benefit you as well. Do not neglect to seek them out.
Sentinel Kendell, at the southeast gate, is again recruiting warriors to battle against the orc tribes that came to our lands from over the Misty Mountains. This time, the plan is to subdue the chief commanders of the orcs. Sentinel Knight Alberius, of the Temple of Eva, is striving to create a fever remedy to cure Adria, who collapsed while studying the North Sea across the Foggy Mountains.
Trader Unoren has been overwhelmed with grief since his companions became spirits of dark magic haunting the Elven Ruins southwest of the village. Perhaps you could do something that might cheer him up.
