Newbie Guide:
You've probably noticed that stores sell Mystic and Fighter weapons separately. Even if they seem to be of the same type, there are subtle differences.
Fighter weapons cause heavy damage but possess low magic attack power, while with Mystic weapons the ratio is reversed. However, Fighter weapons do have some weak magical ability, and if you are a Mystic with a taste for hand-to-hand combat, you might use a sword instead of a staff.
