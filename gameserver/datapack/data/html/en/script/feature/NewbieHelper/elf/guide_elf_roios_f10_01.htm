Newbie Guide:
Now you are ready to venture beyond the immediate vicinity of the village, and begin to explore the deeper parts of the Elven Forest. Continue to improve your equipment and skills with the adena and SP you gain through hunting. This might also be a good time to purchase a bow, if you don't have one already.
And once you reach level 10, it is important to visit Sentinel Kendell. He has a quest for you of great significance.
