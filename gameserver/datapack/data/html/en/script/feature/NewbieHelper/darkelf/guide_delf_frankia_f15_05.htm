Newbie Guide:
Have you completed your mission for Tetrarch Thifiell yet? It is very important that you complete this task.
You should also see Blacksmith Karrod. I suspect he has troubles that need to be dealt with. I'm sure that if you aid him, he'll be most generous.
Look up Trader Minaless as well. She is looking for a supply of zombie dust, and by dealing with her you can increase your profit from hunting marsh zombies.
