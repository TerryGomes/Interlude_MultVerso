Newbie Guide:
Let's see now, which quest is suited to you? You are a formidable fighter now, and it would be a waste to spend your skills on trivial tasks.
Have you finished the quests from Kendell and Alberius? These quests should be easy for you now, and they will provide you with items you should still find useful.
Oh, of course -- Master Ellenia loathes the ratmen, which bring all sorts of diseases to the Elven Forest. She could use your help to eradicate this menace.
