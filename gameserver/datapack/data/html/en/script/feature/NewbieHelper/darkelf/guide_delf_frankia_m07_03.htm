Newbie Guide:
Try hunting imps, ashen wolves or goblins near the village. Your spells are your most powerful attacks, so look for monsters you can defeat with a single spell until you reach at least level 3.
After level 6, go southwest and challenge Balor Orcs or walking fungi. An important rule of thumb at these levels is to hunt the strongest monster you can defeat with two Wind Strikes. And never forget that Orcs are dangerous, because if one is imperiled, others will come to join the fight.
