Newbie Guide:
It is time to discuss forming a party, a group of adventurers to hunt and travel with. Parties are created using Invite in the Action tab (ALT + C) of your Character Status window. First, click on the person you wish to invite into your party, then click the Invite icon. When you next enter the hunting grounds, I recommend you attempt to form a party with another adventurer there.
If you are having difficulty finding party members, use the Party Matching button in the Action tab of your Character Status window. This will display a useful list of adventurers who also wish to form parties. You may use Invite, Whisper or other useful commands from this window.
This is the point in your career where you learn whether you are a "tiger" who likes to hunt alone, or a "lion" who prefers the company of a group. If you're a tiger, you will need to perfect strategies for ensuring your own survival, whereas a lion must learn how how best to serve the interests of the group.
