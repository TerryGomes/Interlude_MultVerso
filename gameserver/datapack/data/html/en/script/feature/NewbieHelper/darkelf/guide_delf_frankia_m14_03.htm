Newbie Guide:
Take the equipment you have -- I hope it includes an Apprentice's Spellbook -- and travel south from the village to hunt Balor Orcs or walking fungi until you are ready for a better weapon.
When you reach Level 9 or 10 and upgrade your weapon, go farther south to the Marshlands or the area surrounding the School of Dark Arts. Try fighting marsh zombies or skeleton hunters there. Be cautious -- Balor Orcs are aggressive, and may attack you before you attack them.
Before you begin hunting orcs, seek out Sentry Kayleen, and speak to Trader Minaless before you start hunting marsh zombies. The quests they give you are necessary preparation.
