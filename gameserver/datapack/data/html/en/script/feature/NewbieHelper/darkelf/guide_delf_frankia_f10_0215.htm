Newbie Guide:
Obviously, the advantage of bows is their ability to deliver damage at range. There is more, though, that you should know. Bows are the slowest weapons, but they can inflict the greatest damage.
A popular tactic is to open combat with a Power Shot, before you close to attack with your main weapon.
Most fighters use their bows as a backup weapon, but with proper training a bow can become your primary means of attack.
