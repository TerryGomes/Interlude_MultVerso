Newbie Guide:
By level 16, if you have been fortunate you may have acquired an apprentice's staff. Even if luck has eluded you so far, you should have enough money to purchase one by level 18.
If you are wearing Stockings of Devotion and a Tunic of Devotion at level 17, you are in excellent shape. Remember to regularly check the goods that other adventurers are offering -- this is the best way to get a bargain price on a desirable item.
There isn't much left to teach you about items, so listen carefully to what I have to say.
