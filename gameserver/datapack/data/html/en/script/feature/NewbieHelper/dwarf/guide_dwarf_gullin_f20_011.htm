Newbie Guide:
A Dwarven Fighter like yourself might choose to become an Artisan, or maybe you'll be a Scavenger. Either way, it's time for you to leave the mines and head out to Aden.
I advise you to go talk to a Grand Master to find out more. He'll examine your qualifications and give you advice on picking your occupation. You can go talk to Warehouse Chief Reed at the warehouse, or Head Blacksmith Bronk at the blacksmith, whichever you'd prefer.
