Newbie Guide:
It's time for you to learn about magic items. I'm sure that by now you've used Lesser Healing Potions, but there's far more that you must know about if you plan to survive.
It's time that you began to acquire accessories. This is the general term for items that increase your magic defense. Soon you will face stone golems in combat, your first magical enemies. If you do not have a strong magic defense, their magic will take you out for sure. So, it's necessary for you to build up your magic defense to prepare for your encounters with stone golems, and other magical foes.
Also, you should seek to acquire the Scroll of Escape. This magic scroll instantly transports you to the nearest village. If your favorite hunting ground is far from the village, the scroll can save you all the travel time used in returning. Remember that even with a scroll, it will still take some time to cast the spell. The scroll isn't cheap at your level, but once you've used it a few times you'll begin to appreciate its value.
