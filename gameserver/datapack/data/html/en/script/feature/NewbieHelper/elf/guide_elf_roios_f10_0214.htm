Newbie Guide:
Dagger type weapons allow you to attack with unsurpassed speed. Furthermore, they have the greatest chance of inflicting critical damage. Their accuracy is low, however, and of course their reach is quite short. Daggers also have a very small range of random damage.
However, in combination with the Mortal Blow skill, a dagger becomes a most appealing choice. Many Rangers, in particular, love daggers for their speed and their ability to deal devastating blows.
