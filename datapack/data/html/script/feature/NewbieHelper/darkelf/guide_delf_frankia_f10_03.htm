Newbie Guide:
I believe Balor Orcs and walking fungi are suitable prey for your skills. They can easily be found in the south reaches of the Dark Forest.
Which reminds me, you must soon complete the Orc Subjugation quest given by Sentry Kayleen. You will gain valuable experience hunting Orcs.
Be very cautious of the marshes to the south; this region may be too dangerous for you. Undead creatures lurk in the swamp. You may be ready for the swamp when you reach Level 11. Until then, I advise you to avoid it.
