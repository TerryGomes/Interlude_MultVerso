Newbie Guide:
Ambitious students of your level should already be familiar with a Long Sword or an Apprentice's Staff. Even if it seems unreachable, you will be able to earn enough to purchase a Long Sword soon.
For armor, start with a Robe of Devotion and Stockings of Devotion, along with a Buckler, Glove, Leather Shoes and a Wooden Helmet. Having all of these items when reaching level 17 should be considered a success. If you have chosen light armor, you will want the Bone Breastplate and Bone Gaiters. However, there are many items that have slightly different properties but are available at similar prices, so pay attention to the items being sold and bought by others.
