Dusk Priestess:
[ Seal of Strife ]
The Seal of Strife bestows the following abilities:
Weak Defensive Mercenaries: The lord of each castle is only allowed to hire raw recruits that are weaker than seasoned mercenaries.
Decreased Strength of Castle Gates and Walls: The strength of castle gates and walls decreases significantly.
Increased in the Cost to Upgrade the Castle Gates and Walls: Upgrading castle gates and walls becomes very expensive.
Reduced Tax Rate Limit: The maximum tax rate is reduced.
Cancel registration of the clans participating in the defense during the siege warfare: the registration of the clans participating in the defense side during the siege warfare is cancelled. However, the registration of the clan that owns the catle and its allies is not canceled.
Cannot ride a Wyvern: During the seal validation period, it is not allowed to ride a Wyvern.
