Newbie Guide:
We Orcs are born fighters, and nobody expects a Mystic like yourself to give up the fun of real fighting. That's why you can choose to hunt mainly with magic, or to fight with a combination of weapons and magic. Either way is fine, it's all up to you.
If you wish to concentrate on magic, use the apprentice's staff until level 5; by then you should be able to save 1,000 adena for an Apprentice's Rod. If you want to stick with weapons, buy a small sword. You might get better armor from the monsters you kill, but that's a matter of luck, so if you're over level 5 and you still don't have gloves, shoes or a helmet, you might want to think about buying the cheapest ones the store offers.
